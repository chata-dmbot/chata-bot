Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to code anything to use this?

Nope. You just describe your bot's personality, connect your Instagram account, and go live. It's built for non-technical users.

What do I need to get started?

An Instagram Business account (very easy to switch), and a Facebook account (guarantees yours and our safety)!

Will this work on my Instagram account?

It currently supports business accounts only, due to Meta's API rules. If you're using a creator or personal account, you can switch to business in your IG settings.

Is this allowed by Instagram?

Yes. We only use the official Instagram Messenger API, which is fully compliant with Meta's terms of service and privacy guidelines.

How fast does the bot reply?

Almost instantly, replies usually go out within 12 seconds of receiving a DM.

What makes this different from other auto-reply tools?

This bot doesn't just respond, it sounds like you. You set its personality, tone, and voice. It can even reference links or promotions you add.

What if I don't know what tone to write?

We provide ready-made tone presets (like chill, confident, playful) and full response templates to help you get started in minutes.

How does the free trial work?

You get 100 free replies with no upfront payment. When those run out, you can decide whether to continue, no auto-charges or surprises.

What happens when I hit my monthly reply limit?

You'll get a warning. You can then top up with +250 replies for 5, or wait until your next month resets.

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, there are no commitments. Cancel or pause from your dashboard whenever you like.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We don't store, sell, or analyze your messages. Replies are generated privately and securely through OpenAI and Meta's infrastructure.
