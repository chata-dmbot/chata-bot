Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Chata and how it works

How does Chata work?

Chata connects to your Instagram account through Meta's official API. When someone sends you a DM, our AI analyzes the message and responds using your personalized settings. Think of it as having a smart assistant who knows exactly how you talk and can handle conversations just like you would.

Is it safe to connect my Instagram account?

Absolutely! We use Instagram's official API and Meta's secure authentication system. We never store your password and only access the permissions you explicitly grant. Your account security is our top priority - we take this very seriously.

Can I customize how Chata responds?

Yes! You can set your bot's personality, tone, and even specific responses for common questions. You can also add custom links, offers, or promotional messages that Chata will share when appropriate. It's like training your own personal assistant.

What if Chata gives a wrong answer?

Chata is designed to be helpful but not perfect. You can review all conversations in your dashboard and manually respond to any messages that need your personal touch. The AI learns from your corrections to improve over time, so it gets better with every interaction.

Can I use Chata for multiple Instagram accounts?

Yes! Our Pro and Creator plans support multiple Instagram accounts. You can set up different personalities and settings for each account, making it perfect for managing multiple brands or personal accounts. Each account gets its own unique voice.

How much does it cost?

We offer three simple pricing tiers: Starter ($29/month), Creator ($49/month), and Pro ($99/month). All plans include a 7-day free trial, and you can cancel anytime. Contact us for full details on our pricing plans.

Do I need technical skills to use Chata?

Not at all! Chata is designed to be super simple. Just connect your Instagram account, set your preferences, and you're ready to go. No coding or technical knowledge required - if you can use Instagram, you can use Chata.

Can I try Chata before paying?

Yes! We offer a free demo that you can try right now. Just click the "Try the Demo" button to see how Chata works with sample conversations. We also offer a 7-day free trial on all paid plans, so you can test it with your real account.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide email support for all users, with priority support for Pro and Creator plans. Our team is here to help you get the most out of Chata and answer any questions you might have. We want you to succeed!

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! We take data privacy seriously. All conversations are encrypted, and we never share your data with third parties. You can delete your data at any time, and we comply with all major privacy regulations. Your privacy is non-negotiable.
