Error: "No Instagram Business account found"

Make sure your Instagram account is:

  • Set to Business type (not Personal or Creator)
  • Connected to a Facebook Page
  • Linked to the Facebook account you're logging in with

Error: "Access denied"

This usually means:

  • Your Instagram account isn't a Business account
  • Your Instagram isn't connected to a Facebook Page
  • You're logging into the wrong Facebook account
